Turnarounds

They are everywhere, and mutually interchangeable.

Here are some quick possibilities:

  • iii-7 vi-7 ii-7 V7
  • Imaj7 vi-7 ii-7 V7
  • Imaj7 V7/ii ii-7 V7
  • iii-7 vi-7 ii-7 Ib7
  • iii-7 vi-7 V7/V7 V7
  • Imaj7 V7/V7 V7sus4 V7 All the regular reharmonization rules apply.

Timba

Timba is a genre of music from Cuba.

Note:I’m not totally sure of everything I write hear. If you want to correct me on something, please do!

Typical timba structure

Songs tend to start with a preview of the montuno section Timba is organized in “gears”. Some of them are:

  • Presión, or pedal: all instruments drop except the piano, or the piano + voice.
  • Marcha arriba, a call and response montuno section between the coro, lead singer. Typically includes cowbell with clave.
  • Mambo: percussion stays on marcha, horns (and/or keyboard).
  • Mazacote: Improvisational percussion section.
  • Bomba: bass+percussion drop, and the bass comes back with those funny slide patterns while tension rises
